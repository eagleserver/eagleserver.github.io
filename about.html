About Us


PRINCIPAL

Roberto Ramirez, AIA was born in El Salvador though grew up just outside Washington DC, in Mount Rainier, MD. He earned a bachelor’s degree in Urban Studies from the University of Maryland at College Park. Subsequently, he went on to earn his Masters of Architecture degree from Morgan State in Baltimore, MD. His career path has given him broad design and technical experiences from health care to commercial office interiors to the world of residential architecture. For over a decade, he has been focused exclusively on residential architecture, initially with a custom design-build firm, then with an award winning residential firm in the nation's capital before establishing RHR Architect in 2014.
